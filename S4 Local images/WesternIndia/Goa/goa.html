Tourist places of Goa

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is a popular beach destination in North Goa, known for its lively atmosphere, beautiful sunsets, and exciting water sports. Located close to Calangute Beach, Baga is a favorite among tourists and locals alike.Location and Geography Baga Beach is situated in the Bardez taluka of North Goa, approximately 30 km north of Panjim. It is bordered by Calangute and Anjuna Beaches, making it a part of the popular beach stretch in North Goa.Beach Activities Baga Beach is famous for its water sports, including parasailing, wakeboarding, windsurfing, kite surfing, and jet skiing. You can also go for dolphin spotting tours and island trips from the beach.
